March 14th

sign up page

The Challenge: Design a sign up page, modal, form, app screen, etc;

I designed a minimalist sign up / sign in page for a movie site I've been working on. This was done in Illustrator using a two colour pallette, and simple vector iconography.

March 15th

credit card checkout

The Challenge: Design a credit card chekout form or page.

A credit card registration form for a premium membership based on having the form resemble an actual card.

March 17th

web calculator

The Challenge: Design a calculator. Standard, scientific, or specialty for something specific.

I wanted to get a bit more creative here, so I decided to do a shipping calculator for an interplanetary package delivery company. I used Illustrator to design, and was happy enough with it that I decided to mess around with CodePen and inline SVG elements. The fully functional pen can be found here.

March 19th

profile page

The Challenge: Design a user profile and be mindful of the most important data, names, imagery, placement etc;

For this, I made a profile page for a UI Dailey challenge site where you can share your designs with the community, and follow other users' progress. You can probably recognize this as the initial inspiration for this blog.

March 22nd

settings menu

The Challenge: Design settings for something. Is it for security or privacy settings? Game settings? What is it, and what's important?

I tried to work with my iconography here and design a simple game settings menu that used icons instead of words for different menu options.

March 23rd

The Challenge: Design a 404 page. Does it suit the brand's style? Is it user-friendly?

It's never fun to land on a 404 error page when browsing a website, so I decided to go for a comedic and fun approach for this challenge.
